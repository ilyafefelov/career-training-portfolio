# Illya Fefelov — Career Portfolio

> **Live Site:** [fefelovcareertraindocs.vercel.app](https://fefelovcareertraindocs.vercel.app/)  
> **GitHub:** [github.com/ilyafefelov/career-training-portfolio](https://github.com/ilyafefelov/career-training-portfolio)

This repository is a curated career portfolio for my transition into MLOps / AI Engineering / LLMOps, with a strong emphasis on applied systems, workflow orchestration, AI integrations, and practical operational value.

## What this repo is for

- Documenting my journey over the past 2 years from full-stack delivery and automation toward MLOps, AI Engineering, and LLMOps.
- Capturing current career positioning, resume variants, LinkedIn drafts, and interview-ready messaging.
- Organizing research and workshop artifacts that support my professional story.
- Providing a public-facing, recruiter-friendly interface to my career materials.
- Preserving legacy materials in `archive/` while keeping the main portfolio clean and easy to review.

## What I am looking for

I am targeting roles that combine engineering, infrastructure, and real AI/ML value delivery, especially in:

- MLOps, AI Engineering, and LLMOps
- AI integrations, automation, and reliable model-driven workflows
- Data infrastructure, ETL, and production-ready analytics pipelines
- Energy, forecasting, decision-support systems, and enterprise/critical infrastructure domains
- Orchestration, observability, and deployment-focused AI solution delivery

I am interested in teams that want practical systems over hype, with a strong product and delivery mindset.

## Repo structure

- **`index.html`** — Bilingual (UA/EN) landing page with navigation to all key materials. Deployed to Vercel.
- **`pages/`** — Rendered HTML pages for LinkedIn profile, professional interests, and MLOps resume.
- **`career-docs/`** — Current career positioning, LinkedIn drafts, post outlines, interview script, and planning notes.
- **`resumes/`** — Resume variants (Markdown and PDF), targeted CV copy, and supporting achievement assets.
- **`research/`** — Published reviews, market study analysis, reflective writing, and research sources.
- **`research/sources/`** — Supporting research PDFs and source documents for review content.
- **`workshops/`** — Career workshop materials, SWOT analysis, and supporting worksheets.
- **`workshops/resources/`** — Workshop reference files, career development PDFs, and planning assets.
- **`workshops/how-to-get-a-raise/`** — SWOT workbooks, development plans, and salary negotiation materials.
- **`projects/final-project/`** — Final project assets and generated retrospective package.
- **`projects/final-project/html_board/`** — Interactive retrospective board (accessible at `/retrospective`).
- **`scripts/`** — Python utility scripts for processing and generating content.
- **`archive/`** — Obsolete or legacy documents that are not part of the main portfolio.

## How to use this repo

**For recruiters and hiring teams:**

1. Start at the **[live portfolio site](https://fefelovcareertraindocs.vercel.app/)** for the cleanest, bilingual presentation.
2. View the **[MLOps Resume page](https://fefelovcareertraindocs.vercel.app/profile/resume-mlops)** for a quick overview.

4. Explore the **[Retrospective Board](https://fefelovcareertraindocs.vercel.app/retrospective)** for project reflection.

**For detailed exploration:**

1. Read `README.md` (this file) first.
2. Review `INDEX.md` for a mapped list of files and the main takeaways.
3. Open `career-docs/` for the most up-to-date career narrative and messaging.
4. Use `resumes/` to view the current CV variants (both Markdown and PDF).
5. Explore `research/` and `projects/` for supporting evidence of analysis and retrospective work.
