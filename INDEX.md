## Why these files matter

- The `career-docs/` folder is the living career narrative: it contains the messaging, positioning, and interview-ready language I am actively using.
- The `resumes/` folder contains the two strongest CV versions for roles in MLOps, AI Engineering, and data infrastructure.
- The `research/` folder demonstrates analytical thinking, market awareness, and an ability to summarize strategic insights.
- The `workshops/` folder shows ongoing professional development and the underlying personal growth process.
- The `projects/final-project/` folder captures a concrete deliverable from my final project work.
- The `archive/` folder preserves earlier artifacts for reference without cluttering the main portfolio.
